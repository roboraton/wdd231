FROG — Website Plan

1. Site Name

Site Name: FROG — Friki Retro Otaku Gamer

This name represents the four pillars of the project (Friki, Retro, Otaku, Gamer) and reflects the theme of geek culture, gaming, anime, and collectibles. It is short, memorable, and fits the project's personality.

Optional domain: frogverse.gg

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to introduce the FROG concept and provide a simple hub for fans of geek culture, gaming, anime, and collectibles. It will include:

  • An explanation of what FROG is.
  • A sample content page showing items related to the FROG theme.
  • A page describing the origin and ideas behind the FROG universe.

3. Scenarios

Possible questions a site visitor may have include:

  • “What is FROG and how do the Friki, Retro, Otaku, and Gamer themes connect?”
  • “Where can I see examples of the type of content or items related to the FROG community?”

4. Color Scheme

The selected color scheme is:

  • Primary: #00FF88 (used for headings, highlights, accents)
  • Secondary: #1B2838 (used for content panels or sections)
  • Background: #050816 (main page background)
  • Text: #F5F5F5 (main text color)

These colors will be applied in the actual website as follows: Primary for headings and highlights, secondary for content panels, rich black for the page background, and white smoke for all body text.

5. Typography

The site will use two fonts:

  • Righteous: used for titles and headings.
  • Poppins: used for body text and general content.

These fonts create a readable but stylized look appropriate for a geek and gaming brand.

In this site plan document, Righteous is used for all headings (h1, h2) and Poppins is used for body text.
