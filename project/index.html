Welcome to the FROG Universe

A hub for fans of geek culture, retro media, anime, manga, and gaming from all eras.

Explore the Four Pillars

  • Friki — general geek culture, comics, sci-fi and fantasy worlds.
  • Retro — nostalgia-driven gaming, media and collectible tech.
  • Otaku — anime, manga, art and Japanese pop culture.
  • Gamer — from classic consoles to modern high-end setups.

Each of these ideas shapes the FROG identity and community.
