The Four Pillars

FROG stands for:

  • Friki – geek culture, movies, series, comics
  • Retro – classic games, nostalgia, old-school tech
  • Otaku – anime, manga, Japan culture
  • Gamer – everything gaming: modern & retro

Purpose of the Site

This website works as an introduction to the FROG concept, providing simple content, sample items, informational sections, and a clean overview of the culture behind it.
