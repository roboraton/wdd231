The Four Pillars

FROG represents four core themes that describe the culture behind the project:

  • Friki — geek culture, movies, series, comics, collectibles.
  • Retro — classic gaming, nostalgia, vintage consoles and media.
  • Otaku — anime, manga, fan art, Japanese pop culture.
  • Gamer — everything gaming: from retro consoles to modern tech.

Purpose of the Site

This website introduces the FROG concept and provides a simple informational hub showing how the four pillars shape the identity. It includes examples, sample content, and a clear explanation of what FROG stands for.
